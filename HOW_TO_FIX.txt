╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                        WHAT WENT WRONG?                               ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Your Supabase database has "Row Level Security" (RLS) policies that control
who can read/write data. One of your policies was checking the "profiles"
table WHILE inserting into the "profiles" table, creating an infinite loop:

    Insert profile
       ↓
    Check RLS policy
       ↓
    Query profiles table  ← This triggers policies again!
       ↓
    Check RLS policy
       ↓
    Query profiles table
       ↓
    ∞ INFINITE LOOP!

The SQL fix replaces the problematic policy with a simple one that doesn't
cause recursion.
